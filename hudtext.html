Codex reveals and lets you extend the layers of structure, notes, and tags which enrich this sBook.

You aren't currently logged in to the sBooks service, which is neccessary to save, share, or overlay glosses (notes, tags, links, etc) on this book. For free content and open sharing, this service is free of charge.

When reading, page through the book by tapping clicking the sides of the screen or swiping horizontally with one finger.

Tap Click the top of the page to see the book tools, the bottom of the page to hide them.

Tap Click a paragraph or heading to see or add notes.

The box at the bottom of the screen shows your location within the book. Tap Click in this box to jump to a different part of the book.

When you see these arrows at the top corners of the page, you're scanning through sections, glosses, or search results. Tap Click the arrows (or the sides of the screen) to go forward and back. You can also swipe with two fingersuse your arrow keys.

Enjoy!
