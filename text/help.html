To move by pages, tap click on the left or right or swipe left or right use the Space/Backspace or PageUp/PageDown keys.
Use the skimmer at the bottom of the page to see and change your location in the book as a whole.
Press, hold, and drag in the skimmer to preview other parts of the book. Let go to return to where you were or press any keytouch anywhere else to stay where you are.
Gloss (highlight) text by pressing on a word until it turns to gold and then move your finger drag the pointer to highlight more words.
Add notes, tags, or links using the form which appears at the top of the page.
Keep them for yourself or share them with your friends, colleagues, or social networks.
Blue gloss bars in the right margin indicate glosses (tags, notes, links, highlights) from you, your networks, or your overlays. Tap Click a gloss bar to see more.
When reading, tap click the top of the page for the book tools which will appear in the four corners:
search semantic tags table of contents
browse glosses contextual help
