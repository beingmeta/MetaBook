Login to sBooks

To use many of the features of Codex, you need to be logged into an sBooks account. A beingmeta sBooks account is free and lets you save and share your glosses (notes, links, tags, etc) on the books you read. Your account lets you create a network of friends or connecto to your existing social network and gives you control over who sees the glosses you add your books and also allows you to select the layers and conversations which will be included in your personal sBooks.
