Welcome to sBooks

sBooks are digital books personally customized with comments, tags, or links from your friends, communities, and networks as well as (selectively) experts, critics, or teachers.

Welcome to Codex, the sBooks e-reader

You're using Codex, a web-based e-reader created to deepen reading and engagement while connecting to networks of knowledge, conversation, and commmunity. Codex aims to reclaim the virtues of physical books, making it natural to navigate, annotate, search, and personalize electronic books.

Tap Click on the left or right to move by pages. You can also swipe left or right.
Tap Click on the top to bring up the interface (or just swipe up or down).
Tap Click on the bottom to return to the text.
Press and hold Click and hold the mouse to highlight text and add your own glosses: notes, tags, and links. Keep them for yourself or share them with your friends, colleagues, or social networks.
Tap Click the blue bars in the margin to view existing glosses added by yourself, your community, or any of the overlays you've applied to your book.
Tap Click in the corners to:
Browse by chapters or sections. Search by tags, phrases, or concepts
Toggle contextual help (like this) for whatever you're doing Browse people's glosses (notes, links, tags, etc)
