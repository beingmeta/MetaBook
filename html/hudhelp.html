Ok Searching tags and text

You are searching for passages or glosses within your book, using either semantic tags or literal terms. As you type or edit the text in the search box, the cloud of tags and phrases will grow or shrink to display matching phrases, tags, or tag synonyms.

Tapping Clicking a term or tag adds it to the current search, generating both new results and an updated cloud of associated terms and tags. Tags may be related to other tags (for example, dachshund to dog) and those relationships are used in your search.

The results of your search are ordered by relevance and position within the book. Refine or change your search by selecting one of the tabs ('co-tags' or 'all tags') above the results or by simply entering more text. Co-tags are tags which occur among the current search results; all tags are all tags used throughout the book.

Ok The Table of Contents

The table of contents (TOC) displays a hierarchical map of your book created by the author or publisher. Red lines indicate your current reading location; the light-colored bars indicate the subsection's size and position within its parent.

Use the space and backspace keys to move through the TOC page-by-page; combine with the shift key or use the arrow keys to move in larger increments. You can also use the percentage indicator to move page-by-page or enter a specific percentage (just click on the number).

Swipe left or right to move page-by-page through the TOC; swipe with two fingers to move in larger increments. You can also use the percentage indicator to move forward or backward or to enter a specific percentage (just tap on the number).

Tap Click a line in the TOC to jump to that section. You'll start skimming through the table of contents while seeing the content of the book.
