Ok Searching tags and text

You are searching for passages or glosses within your book, using either semantic tags or literal terms. As you type or edit in the search box, the cloud of tags and phrases will grow or shrink to display matching phrases, tags, or synonyms of tags.

Tapping Clicking adds a term or tag to the current search, generating both results and an updated cloud of terms and tags. Tags may be related to other tags (for example, dachshund to dog) and those relationships are used in your search.

Once you have specified at least one tag or term, you can see the matching results by either tapping clicking on the result count or using the Enter key in an empty input box. If there are few enough results, it will switch to them automatically.
