sBooks are digital books enriched by layers of structure, semantics, and annotations from your friends, colleagues, and chosen communities.

Glosses are notes, tags, and links overlaid on the content of this book and indicated by tappable clickable icons in the text or margins.

Login to use the annotation and social features of sBooks. For free content and open communities, this service is free of charge.

This book is personalized with glosses for Somebody Interesting. If that's not you, please logout; if it is you, you can view and update your profile here.

Flip forward and backward through pages by tapping clicking the sides of the display,swiping right or left, or using the space, backspace, or page up/down buttons on your keyboard.

Add glosses of your own by selecting text and then adding a note, tags, or links using the dialog at the top of the page.

Jump through the book by tapping clicking in the linear book map at the bottom of the page.

Tap the top Click on the top of the page to see book tools providing extra information, settings, and other options.

Tap the bottom Click on the bottom of the page to return to the text.
