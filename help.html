Welcome to Codex, an sBooks reader

sBooks are digital books enriched by layers of structure, semantics, and annotations from your friends, colleagues, and chosen communities.

Login to use the annotation and social features of sBooks. For free content and open communities, this service is free of charge.

This book is personalized for Somebody Interesting. If that's not you, please logout; if it is you, you can view and update your profile here.

Tap Click the bottom of the page to see the book and the top of the page to use the tools. The 'H' key or the icon (up top) displays this help screen.

Flip through pages by tapping clicking the sides of the display,swiping right or left, or using the space, backspace, or page up/down buttons on your keyboard.

Reveal the glosses (notes, tags, links, etc) added to the book by clicking on the icons in the text; reveal all of the overlaid glosses by clicking on the overlays icon in the lower right.

Jump through the book by tapping clicking in the progress display at the bottom of the page; tap click on on the page number or the location (Lddd) to jump to a particular location within the text.

Annotate passages with your own glosses by either selecting some text or tapping clicking a paragraph and using the icon which appears to its left.

Navigate the book's structure by clicking on the navigation icon in the upper left; a panel at the top of the page discloses the arrangement and sizes of the book's elements.

Search using the search icon in the upper right to find passages or sections based on tags assigned to by your community, the author/publisher, or helpful robots.

Scan through the book by clicking the double arrow icons, swiping with two fingers, or using the arrow keys. Scanning can advance based on section structure, search results, or annotations (glosses).
